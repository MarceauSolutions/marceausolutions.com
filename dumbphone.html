Questions

Is this just another Screen Time app?
No. Screen Time is easy to bypass. DumbPhone Lock uses Apple's FamilyControls API to actually prevent app access — the same system parents use to lock their kids' phones. You can't just "ignore the limit."
Can I still get calls and texts?
Yes. Phone, Messages, and FaceTime always work. You choose exactly which apps stay accessible.
What if I need an app in an emergency?
You can end a focus session early — but the app adds intentional friction (confirmation dialogs) so you don't cave on impulse.
