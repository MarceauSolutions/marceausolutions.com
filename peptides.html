Peptides: Cutting Through the Hype

Most peptide information online is either overhyped marketing or sketchy gray market advice. I take a different approach: work with physicians, use FDA-approved compounds, and track everything.

What Are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules in your body. Some can influence growth hormone, recovery, sleep, and body composition.

But here's the thing: not all peptides are created equal. The evidence varies wildly from compound to compound. Some have decades of clinical research. Others have basically zero human data.

My job is to help you understand the difference and make informed decisions with physician oversight.
