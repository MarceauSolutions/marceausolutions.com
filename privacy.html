Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Marceau Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI automation services, website, and SMS marketing programs.

This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name
  • Account Information: Username, password, billing information
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and support requests
  • SMS Opt-In Data: Mobile phone number, consent records, opt-in/opt-out timestamps

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, click patterns
  • Cookies: Session cookies, analytics cookies, preference cookies
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address

2.3 AI-Generated Data

  • Video Processing: Video files uploaded for editing (jump cuts, silence removal)
  • Graphics: Text and images for educational content creation
  • Email Data: Email metadata for digest generation (with your explicit consent via Google OAuth)
  • Analytics: Revenue and expense data from connected Google Sheets

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Process your AI automation requests, video editing, graphics creation
  • SMS Marketing: Send promotional messages, updates, and alerts (only with explicit consent)
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, process payments
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns and improve our services
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Marketing: Send promotional emails (with opt-out options)

4. SMS Marketing & TCPA Compliance

4.1 Express Written Consent

Before sending you SMS messages, we obtain your express written consent as required by the TCPA. Your consent includes:

  • Agreement to receive automated marketing messages from Marceau Solutions
  • Understanding that message and data rates may apply
  • Acknowledgment that consent is not required to purchase services
  • Knowledge of how to revoke consent at any time

4.2 Message Frequency

We send approximately 4-8 promotional messages per month. Frequency may vary based on your engagement and promotional campaigns.

4.3 Opt-Out Rights (Effective April 11, 2025)

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time through any reasonable means, including:

  • Replying "STOP," "CANCEL," "UNSUBSCRIBE," or similar keywords to any message
  • Sending an email to support@marceausolutions.com
  • Calling 1-800-MARCEAU
  • Using any informal message like "Leave me alone" or "No more texts"

We will process your opt-out request within 10 days and confirm your unsubscription via SMS.

4.4 Quiet Hours

We do not send SMS messages before 8:00 AM or after 9:00 PM in your local time zone, as required by TCPA regulations.

4.5 Record Retention

We retain consent and opt-out records for 4 years to comply with TCPA statute of limitations.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Google APIs (Gmail, Calendar, Sheets), Twilio (SMS), Grok/xAI (image generation), Railway (hosting)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe or other payment gateways (for billing)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, subpoena, or court order
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

6.2 CCPA/CPRA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of collected personal information
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: Control how we use sensitive data

To exercise your rights: Email privacy@marceausolutions.com or call 1-800-MARCEAU

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Employee training on data privacy and security

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., 4 years for SMS consent records)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Account data is retained for 12 months after account closure unless you request earlier deletion.

9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Enable core website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

10. Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with third-party platforms:

  • Google APIs: Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Sheets - Google Privacy Policy
  • Twilio: SMS messaging - Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Grok/xAI: AI image generation - xAI Privacy Policy
  • Railway: Application hosting - Railway Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their policies.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure adequate safeguards through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with service providers
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • SMS notification (for significant changes affecting SMS subscribers)

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
