The Backstory

A traumatic brain injury changed everything. One moment I was living my life; the next, I was starting over from zero. Physically broken. Mentally foggy. The kind of setback that makes most people question whether they'll ever feel normal again.

I didn't just want normal. I wanted stronger.

Recovery wasn't linear and it sure wasn't fast. But it taught me something most people never learn the hard way: your body — and your business — is an incredibly adaptive system when you give it the right inputs. The problem is that most advice out there is built on trends, not evidence. I needed something that actually worked.

So I went deep. I studied evidence-based training methodologies. I learned how the body actually recovers and rebuilds. I dove into technology, automation, and AI. I stopped listening to surface-level advice and started reading the research, building the systems, and testing what actually moved the needle.

Piece by piece, I rebuilt myself — stronger than I was before.

Why Multiple Services

People sometimes ask why I do AI, web development, fitness coaching, AND work with creators. The answer is simple: they're all connected by the same core skill.

I figure out how systems work and make them better.

Whether that's building an AI automation that saves a business 20 hours a week, designing a website that actually converts, creating a training program that delivers results, or helping a creator build real revenue — the underlying approach is identical. Understand the system, identify the bottlenecks, apply evidence-based solutions, and iterate.

The technology changes. The methodology doesn't.
