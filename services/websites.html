Common Questions

Why not use WordPress or Squarespace? +
Hand-coded sites load 3-5x faster, rank better in Google, and don't have plugin vulnerabilities. Your site will score 90+ on Google PageSpeed. No bloated themes, no monthly plugin updates that break things, no security patches to worry about.
How long does it take? +
Starter: 1 week. Professional: 2 weeks. Premium: 3 weeks. These timelines start after the design is approved. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
Do you do e-commerce? +
Yes. We can integrate Stripe checkout, product pages, and shopping carts. Whether you're selling physical products, digital downloads, or services, I'll build a checkout flow that converts.
What about hosting? +
GitHub Pages (free) or your preferred host. No monthly hosting fees with our default setup. Your site lives on fast, reliable infrastructure with zero hosting costs.
Can I make changes myself? +
Content updates are included in the maintenance retainer. Or I can teach you basic HTML editing so you can make simple text and image changes on your own. Either way, you're never stuck.
