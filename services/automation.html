Case Study

SW Florida Comfort HVAC

The owner was losing 30-40% of incoming calls. After-hours emergencies went straight to voicemail. His office manager was overwhelmed juggling phones, scheduling, and follow-ups.

Then we deployed Voice AI. A system that answers every call, asks the right questions, books appointments, and routes emergencies instantly.

Within 30 days: 312 calls handled by AI. 18 new customers acquired. 47 appointments booked automatically. The office manager got 18 hours per week back to focus on operations instead of being chained to the phone.
