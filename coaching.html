Common Questions

What equipment do I need? +
I can work with whatever you have access to. Full gym? Great. Home setup with dumbbells and bands? Also great. I'll design your program around your available equipment.
How much time do I need to commit? +
Most clients train 3-5 days per week for 45-90 minutes. But I can design programs for 3x30min if that's what your schedule allows. Consistency beats perfection.
What if I'm a complete beginner? +
Perfect. Beginners often make the fastest progress because there are no bad habits to unlearn. I'll teach you proper form and build a strong foundation from day one.
What's the difference between the programs? +
Mainly commitment length and support level. Starter is great for building foundations. Performance adds more frequent check-ins and deeper analytics. Elite includes peptide consultation with physician oversight for advanced optimization.
Do you guarantee results? +
I guarantee my effort, expertise, and commitment to your progress. But results require your consistency too. If you follow the program, you will see results. That's not hype‚Äîit's how training works.
