## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Informed skeptic, evidence-based, no-BS

**Do Say:**
- "Research shows..."
- "Track everything"
- "Start conservative"
- "Work with professionals"
- "Optimize the basics first"

**Don't Say:**
- "Miracle results"
- "Quick fix"
- "No side effects"
- Specific dosing recommendations
- Vendor recommendations

**Compliance:**
- "Not medical advice" on every peptide page
- "Consult your healthcare provider" prominent
- No before/after health claims
- No specific dosing
